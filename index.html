About Us

The ACM Club is a vibrant community of computer science students dedicated to exploring the latest technological advancements. We offer a platform for members to expand their knowledge through workshops and seminars led by industry experts, sharpen their coding skills through challenges and competitions, and build valuable connections with peers. Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just starting your tech journey, join us to discover exciting career opportunities and shape the future of technology together.

Want to join?

Are you passionate about computer science and eager to be part of a dynamic community? We invite you to apply to join the ACM Club. To submit your application, please provide a completed application form, a copy of your academic transcript, and a letter outlining your interest in the club and your potential contributions. We seek enthusiastic students who are eager to learn, collaborate, and grow within the field of computer science.
